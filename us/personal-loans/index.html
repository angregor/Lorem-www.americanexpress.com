Looking for a Business Loan?
American Express Personal Loans are available to pre-approved Card Members only. Find out today if you are pre-approved. To learn more, see Terms and Conditions.
*You must be pre-approved for a loan offer in order to apply. Your pre-approved APR and loan amount are based on your creditworthiness and other factors. Pre-approved APRs will be between 6.90% and 19.97%, as of 2/1/2017. Not all customers will be pre-approved for the lowest APR or the highest loan amount. The repayment periods are 12, 24 or 36 months. If you are approved for and accept an American Express Personal Loan, we will report the loan information to credit bureaus, which may have an impact on your credit score. A representative example of the cost of an American Express Personal Loan is: For a $10,000 loan repaid over 36 months at an 8.98% APR, the monthly payment amount would be $317.90, the total interest would be $1,445, and the total cost would be $11,445 (original loan amount plus interest). Actual rates, interest and costs may vary.
